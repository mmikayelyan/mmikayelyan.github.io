FOOD AND DRINK BANNER IMPACT ON E-COMMERCE REVENUE

A/B TEST ANALYSIS: ASSESSING CONVERSION RATE AND REVENUE IMPACT OF A FOOD AND DRINK BANNER ON AN E-COMMERCE WEBSITE


Description

This project delves into an A/B test conducted by an e-commerce company, GloBox, to assess the effectiveness of a food and drink banner on their mobile website. The analysis focuses on key metrics, including the Conversion Rate and Average Revenue Per User. The results reveal a statistically significant increase in the conversion rate for the test group but no significant difference in average revenue per user. The recommendation advises against launching the experience to all users, emphasizing the need for iterative improvements to banner design, placement, and messaging. The project highlights the potential for future testing and data-driven decision-making.

CHURN ANALYSIS OF THE BANK CLIENTS

UNVEILING CUSTOMER CHURN PATTERNS IN THE BANK'S CREDIT CARD SERVICES: INSIGHTS, TRENDS, AND STRATEGIES TO RETAIN VALUABLE CLIENTS


Description

This project presents a comprehensive churn analysis of the Bank's credit card services, aiming to understand and address the increasing customer attrition. Explore the dataset containing 10,127 data points across 21 features, including customer information, bank relationship details, and service usage. Gain insights into the top reasons for churn and actionable recommendations to prevent customer attrition. From analyzing average utilization ratios and contact frequencies to examining product relationships and card categories, uncover valuable strategies to enhance customer retention and drive bank growth.
